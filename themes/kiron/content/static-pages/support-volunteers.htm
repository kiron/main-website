We're working hard to provide refugees worldwide with access to higher education. There are many ways for you to support us. Here are 5 easy things for you to start immediately:

  • Like us on Facebook and invite your friends to do the same
  • Tell your friends about us and raise awareness
  • Tell your local refugee organisation about us
  • Tell your educational institution about us
  • Tell your local newspaper/politician about us

Kiron is an extremely international institution with students from more than 60 countries and with even more diverse cultural backgrounds. Our team reflects this international buzzing atmosphere. We are working with people from all over the world, combining educational diversity and international experience with start-up expertise and passion for the idea to give refugees access to higher education.

Our team members and volunteers continue to be one of our key strengths: everyone comes with diverse experiences, yet, is driven by the same passion to empower refugees around the world. Kiron offers a range of volunteering and internship opportunities in our head office in Berlin as well as in our teams in Istanbul and London.

If you would like to contribute to the vision of Kiron, please check the following vacancies and options. Then shoot us an email (join.us@kiron.ngo) your CV attached.

Programmers Wanted!

Our core offering to our students is the Student Portal, a growing javascript web application built on state of the art technologies. Our stack is a React.js frontend using a Sails.js backend, manipulating our PostgreSQL database. We mostly need Frontend/UX programmers, but if you can help anywhere along the line, please let us know. We are always looking for new programmers to join our team. We expect knowledge in common techniques (e.g. OOP) and tools (e.g. git) from all developers. We are looking for support as well for server administration, building up smaller self contained apps, PHP programmers ideally familiar with Laravel, Database wizardy and architecture development.

Country manager

Refugees face problems to access higher education all over the world. Kiron is an international instutions and aims to provide a global solution to this problem. Therefore, we want to establish small regional teams that connect with national NGO`s working with refugees in order to inform them about Kiron Universtiy.

This includes also to connect with national media, government and the civil society in order to encourage as many people and institutions as possible to take action. We currently have teams working in Berlin, London and Istanbul but we would love to hear back from you if you would like to help us to set up a regional group in your home country. Please contact us with an email containing your CV and an outline of your direct contacts to relevant key players in your country.
