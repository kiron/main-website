Head of Communications

Start: June - Office: Berlin - fulltime

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with the mission to enable access to higher education and successful learning for refugees through digital solutions. As part of an innovative educational model Kiron offers coherent curricula using MOOCs (Massive Open OnlineCourses) from partner platforms like Coursera and edX so that refugees can start studying regardless of their status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 70 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:

  • Towards the public: Run communication campaigns to raise awareness to the general refugee situation and raise Kirons brand awareness.

  • Towards our stakeholders: Manage Kiron´s international, multi-stakeholder communication in a politically challenging environment via various channels including communication with businesses, academic institutions and funders.

  • Towards our team: Improve our internal documentation and develop strategic controlling and processes for Marketing and Communications.

  • Towards potential students: Be responsible for student outreach including joint communication efforts with other NGO´s. Your communication goal will be to inspire and motivate potential students to join Kirons academic program.

Your set of skills:

  • You are a positive, mindful and empathetic communicator able to balance our communications to multiple stakeholders with a focus on our students, ideally through own personal or work-related experiences in the field

  • You have at least 5 years experience as a leader of a multi-disciplinary team including Design, PR, Internal Communication, Social Media and International Marketing Management

  • You bring in a network of NGO contacts that are working with refugees and migrants

  • You can organise professional content production workflows

  • You are able to communicate professionally with an international team with English as the main language (besides German, French, Turkish and Arabic)

Our offer:

  • The opportunity to shape a social enterprise with your initiative and creative input

  • The opportunity to lead an interdisciplinary international team of 5-10 people

  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup and the chance to be part of a dynamic team

  • The possibility to join our management board.

  • Flexible working hours at one of our locations.
