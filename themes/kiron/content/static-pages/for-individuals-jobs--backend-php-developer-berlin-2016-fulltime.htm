Backend (PHP) Developer

Start: September 2016 - Office: Berlin - fulltime

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with a mission to remove the barriers refugees face in accessing higher education. Through an innovative model of blended learning, Kiron offers tailor-made curricula of MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) through renowned educational platforms like coursera and edx so that refugees can start studying, regardless of their asylum status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 40 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:.

  • Contribute to the mobile-first single page web applications being built.

  • Responsibility for feature development in the Kiron Platforms.

  • Work with the rest of the developer team and others to find lightweight solutions.

  • Take responsibility for the features and services you are building.

Your set of skills:

  • You possess a strong background in PHP and a familiarity with modern frontend javascript.
    frameworks. We currently use React and Redux, with a split backend using Sails.js and
    Laravel PHP.

  • You understand the architecture of a single page web application.

  • You have experience working with 3rd party open source tools which integrate into a custom
    stack. We currently use Discourse, LimeSurvey and Metabase, for example.

  • You are a team player and value open communication.

  • Your English skills should be excellent, basic German is a plus.

Our offer:

  • The experience to lead a highly committed, international and interdisciplinary (volunteer)
    team.

  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup with several stakeholders
    (refugees, corporations, NGOs, higher education and public sector institutions).

  • Regular team building as well as representative events, mentoring, trainings and workshops.

  • The opportunity to design the vision of Kiron through your own creative thinking and ambition.

  • A competitive salary within the social sector.

  • Flexible working hours (incl. Home Office) at our Berlin location.
