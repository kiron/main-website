How to Collaborate as an Academic institution

Kiron offers different paths to partnership with academic institutions. Overall, the possible partnership avenues with Kiron boil down to the following three main aspects: 1) Development and recognition of our study program, 2) Curriculum development (encompasses Direct Academics program and MOOC development), and  3) Additional ways to collaborate, which includes development and implementation of Study hubs, Research, and partaking in Competence pool.
