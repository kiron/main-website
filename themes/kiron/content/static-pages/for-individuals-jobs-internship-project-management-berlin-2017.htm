Internship Student Services Project Management

Start: February or later - Offices: Berlin - fulltime

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with the mission to enable access to higher education and successful learning for refugees through digital solutions. As part of an innovative educational model Kiron offers coherent curricula using MOOCs (Massive Open OnlineCourses) from partner platforms like Coursera and edX so that refugees can start studying regardless of their status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 70 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:

  • You are part of the Student Services Team in Berlin

  • You are in charge of executing a project that aims to place students into partner universities in Germany & France

  • You are a contact person for Kiron’s academically most successful students 

  • You support the development and implementation of Kiron’s long term strategy in transferring Kiron students to partner universities

Your set of skills:

  • You are an university student and share the vision of free education worldwide for refugees

  • You have an entrepreneurial spirit and a hands on mentality

  • You are fluent in English and German (French and/or Arabic is a plus)

  • You are proficient in the MS Office Package

  • First knowledge about legal admission criteria for refugees to German and/or French Higher Education is a plus 

Our offer:

  • The opportunity to shape a social enterprise with your creative input

  • The insight into the “heart” of Kiron – by working on the transfer of our students to partner institutions

  • High level of autonomous working

  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup and the chance to be part of a dynamic, international, and interdisciplinary team at a great office location in Berlin Mitte

  • Investment in you: Regular team building and representative events, mentoring, trainings and workshops

Ready to join our mission? Apply now!

We are looking forward to your application via e-mail with the subject “Internship StuServ Project Mgmt  + your name” to Samira Helbig at internship@kiron.ngo. Include your cover letter, CV and availability in English or German in a single PDF file.

Please note that due to our limited budget as an nonprofit organization we are unfortunately unable to pay salaries. Therefore we are only offering mandatory internships/ educational program (“Pflichtpraktikum”) or study orientation internships between bachelor and master (“Orientierungspraktikum”).

If you want to support Kiron but none of the above mentioned internship types fit your current status, please get in touch with us and we can tell you more about volunteering options.
