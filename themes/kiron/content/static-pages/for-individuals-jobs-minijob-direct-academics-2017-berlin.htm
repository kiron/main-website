Minijob in Direct Academics

Start: January 2017 - Office: Berlin

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with a mission to remove the barriers refugees face in accessing higher education. Through an innovative model of blended learning, Kiron offers tailor-made curricula of MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) through renowned educational platforms like coursera and edx so that refugees can start studying, regardless of their asylum status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 70 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:

  • Playing a key role in developing student relations/community management for the Direct
  • Academics department
  • Identifying creative ways to engage and maintain students in our courses
  • Promoting our courses through student communications and social media
  • Supporting design processes for recruiting students and volunteer instructors
  • Supporting the DA Team in the coordination and administration of tutorials
  • Supporting in partnership work as needed

Your set of skills:

  • You are a self-starter, hard-working, adaptive, & have an entrepreneurial mind-set
  • You have excellent English written and oral skills and ideally you are a native German speaker
  • You are outgoing, creative and passionate about communication, and are enthusiastic about trying out new techniques and approaches
  • You have a key eye for design and like playing around with presentation tools
  • You have experience or a strong interest in marketing and sales
  • You share the vision of free worldwide education for refugees

Our offer:

  • 450€ monthly salary, 45 hrs/month, flexible working hours
  • Opportunity to shape a social enterprise with your initiative and creative input
  • Insight into the development of online and digital learning content in a social start-up.
  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup and the chance to be part of a dynamic, international, and interdisciplinary team
  • Attractive office location in Berlin-Mitte
