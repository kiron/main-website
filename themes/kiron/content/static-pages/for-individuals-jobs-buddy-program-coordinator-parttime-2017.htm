Peer Program Coordinator

Part-time or Full-time in Berlin, starting March 2017

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with the mission to enable access to higher education and successful learning for refugees through digital solutions. As part of an innovative educational model Kiron offers coherent curricula using MOOCs (Massive Open OnlineCourses) from partner platforms like Coursera and edX so that refugees can start studying regardless of their status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 70 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:

  • Recruitment of students interested in joining Kiron’s peer programs
  • Pairing Kiron students with fitting local students and corporate mentors
  • Conducting online and offline trainings for peers and Kiron students
  • Maintaining frequent and steady contact with the program participants
  • Coordinating the collaboration of several regional teams with the Berlin Headquarter
  • Supporting the student services team in several areas of our operations
  • Partnering colleagues to improve student services for all students

Your set of skills:

  • You have a Master’s degree and at least 2 years of relevant work experience, ideally in a social start-up or NGO
  • You have rigorous organizational and project management skills paired with empathic communicative skills
  • You are a self-starter and able to work independently and conscientiously
  • You have a strong motivation to empower our students to succeed personally and academically and build an intercultural community.
  • Fluency in English, German is a plus

Our offer:

  • Opportunity to shape an innovative online and offline peer programs with your initiative and creative input.
  • A competitive salary in the social sector.
  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup and the chance to be part of a dynamic, international, and interdisciplinary team.
  • Investment in you: Regular team building and representative events, mentoring, trainings and workshops
  • Attractive office location in Berlin-Mitte.
