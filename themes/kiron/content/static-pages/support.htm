Kiron's vision is to provide refugees worldwide with the opportunity to graduate from an accredited university, free of charge. This ambitious project can become a reality only with your support.

In less than a year, and with the help of many dedicated individuals and institutions, Kiron has given more than 1000 students access to higher education. To extend this opportunity to many more refugees, and to enhance our existing services, we are constantly looking for motivated volunteers, strong partners, and financial support.
