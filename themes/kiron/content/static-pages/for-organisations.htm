Kiron for Organisations

Welcome to Kiron!

We are a social startup delivering higher education to refugees. In our opinion, everyone has the right to fulfill their potential and gain the tools for success through access to quality education. We know that the obstacles refugees face in accessing higher education can be overcome, and we are working hard to do so.

Our vision is to provide millions of refugees worldwide with the opportunity to graduate with an accredited university degree, free of charge. No more time, potential, or lives wasted.

We are at the forefront of driving innovation in education and migration solutions. In order to achieve our goals, we strongly depend on a network of reliable partners to develop our approach of providing easily accessible education for refugees.
