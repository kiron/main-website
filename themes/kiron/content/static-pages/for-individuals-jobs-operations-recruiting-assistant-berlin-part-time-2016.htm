People Operations & Recruiting Assistant

Start: December 2016 - Offices: Berlin - part-time

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with a mission to remove the barriers refugees face in accessing higher education. Through an innovative model of blended learning, Kiron offers tailor-made curricula of MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) through renowned educational platforms like coursera and edx so that refugees can start studying, regardless of their asylum status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 40 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:.

  • Supporting the needs assessment as well as the channel posting of open positions

  • Leading operative applicant management in Kiron’s HR Management Software Personio

  • Conducting phone interviews with job applicants & matching conversations with volunteers

  • Talent scouting and establishment of an international career and volunteer pool

  • Leading regular onboarding and offboarding processes

  • Preparing and conducting team trainings and events

  • Providing administrative support for the Organization Department

Your set of skills:

  • You have a Bachelor’s Degree in any relevant field of studies and  at least 1 years of work experience in HR Management, ideally with a focus on Recruiting and/or HR Processes

  • You are a self-starter, hard-working, adaptive, & have an entrepreneurial mind-set 

  • You are completely outstanding at building up and maintaining personal relationships

  • You have excellent German and English writing and oral skills, are proficient with Microsoft Office Package, Google Apps, and Social Media

  • You share the vision of free worldwide education for refugees

Our offer:

  • The opportunity to shape a social enterprise with your initiative and creative input

  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup and the chance to be part of a dynamic, international, and interdisciplinary team

  • A competitive salary within the social sector

  • Flexible working hours at one of our locations

  • Investment in you: Regular team building and representative events, mentoring, trainings and workshops.
