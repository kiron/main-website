Core Curricula and Prep Courses

Each study track consists of one common core curriculum and facultative preparatory courses. To account for the huge diversity in academic backgrounds of our students, we are offering track-specific prep courses to bridge the gap from the individual student's knowledge to the level needed to follow our university-level courses. These courses are facultative but strongly recommended for the students to avoid frustration with too difficult courses of the core curriculum. We are currently working on entry-assessments to guide each student in the appropriate course-level.
