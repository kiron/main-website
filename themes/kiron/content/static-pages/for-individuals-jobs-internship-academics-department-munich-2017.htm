Internship Academics Department

Start: January 2017 - Office: Munich


Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with a mission to remove the barriers refugees face in accessing higher education. Through an innovative model of blended learning, Kiron offers tailor-made curricula of MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) through renowned educational platforms like coursera and edx so that refugees can start studying, regardless of their asylum status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 70 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:.

  • Acquisition and management of partnerships with open distance universities; 

  • Building, acquiring, and strengthening relationships with external stakeholders and potential partners which could bring new funding, networking, and academic opportunities for Kiron;

  • Assisting the International Academics Manager in the daily administrative activities.

  • Working together with the management team on the long-term academics strategy

  • Translating leadership goals into actionable strategic initiatives

  • Taking responsibility for projects depending on your preferences & skills

Your set of skills:

  • You are a student and you share the vision of free education worldwide for refugees

  • You are fluent in English and have excellent written communication skills

  • You are used to the MS Office Package

  • You are a self-starter and think like a social entrepreneur, you are an excellent communicator, and motivated to work hard 

Our offer:

  • The opportunity to shape a social enterprise with your initiative and creative input

  • The insight in the development of an Academic department in a social start-up.

  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup and the chance to be part of a dynamic, international, and interdisciplinary team

  • High level of autonomous working 

We are looking forward to your application via e-mail with the subject “Intern_Academics  + your name” to Samira Helbig at internship@kiron.ngo. Include your cover letter, CV and availability in English or German in a single PDF file.

Please note that due to our limited budget as an nonprofit organization we are unfortunately unable to pay salaries. Therefore we are only offering mandatory internships/ educational program (“Pflichtpraktikum”) or study orientation internships between bachelor and master (“Orientierungspraktikum”).

If you want to support Kiron but none of the above mentioned internship types fit your current status, please get in touch with us and we can tell you more about volunteering options.
