Business & Economics

The Business & Economics core curriculum offers the best of both worlds: In our Economics modules, you will understand on a theoretical level how modern economies function and integrate into a larger social framework, while in our Business modules you will learn on a practical level how to lead a business organization. Going through our Business & Economics track and completing your degree at a partner university equips you with a set of both intellectual and hands-on skills that make you a particularly valuable job market candidate.

Key Learning Outcomes

In this study track, you begin by learning the basics of both Business Administration and Economics, including for example foundations in maths & statistics, marketing, accounting, human resources, and the forces that drive our economy on a macro and micro level.

After successfully completing the Business & Economics curriculum,

  • you make informed decisions that secure sustainable business success in a complex and fast-paced environment,

  • you apply economic theory to the solution of real-world economic problems, and

  • you gain core transferable skills (e.g. using quantitative methods and model thinking) that help you succeed in any future career.

Where will the degree take you?

A degree in the field of Business & Economics offers excellent career opportunities across sectors, ranging from governmental institutions to private companies. Furthermore, a Bachelor’s degree in the field of Business & Economics qualifies you for a variety of Master’s degrees worldwide. After specializing, there are a number of career paths open to you, for example in Human Resources, Marketing, Accounting, or Consultancy.
