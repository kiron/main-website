Our student service team is here to help you with any problems you might have during your application or studies. We offer many support services to help you have a fun and productive time studying with us.



Language Services: Our language services team is here to help you get to the required language level in order to succeed with your studies. In cooperation with partners such as Lingua TV, Babbel and the Language Testing Service, we provide many different language options for you in German and English.

Buddy Programme: Feeling a bit lost in your new city, or wishing there was someone you could ask for advice about your studies? Don't worry - our buddy programme is here to help you out. We match you with friendly and supportive students from local universities, who will give you help and advice and introduce you to the student life in your city!

Mentoring Programme: Our mentoring programme is a fantastic opportunity for you to meet and connect with professionals who work in the field you are interested in. Mentors start by helping you prepare for your studies and offering vocational orientation, and eventually provide you with internships and other practical experiences within a corporation.

Psychological Services: We understand that studying is probably not the biggest problem you are facing right now. If you are struggling to cope, or would just like to talk to somebody, you can get in touch with our trained psychologists for support.

Career Services: You can use the Kiron Career Platform to gather general information about scholarships, funding opportunities and further education.
