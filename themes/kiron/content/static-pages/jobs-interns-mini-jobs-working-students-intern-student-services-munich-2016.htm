Intern in Student Services (m/f)

to support our operations in Munich Start:15th of july or later (ongoing).

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with a mission to remove the barriers refugees face in accessing higher education. Through an innovative model of blended learning, Kiron offers tailor-made curricula of MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) through renowned educational platforms like coursera and edx so that refugees can start studying, regardless of their asylum status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 40 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:

  • Buddy-Programme: help improve our buddy-program where our students are matched with students from other universities to help them accommodate to life in Germany (supervision, evaluation, tandem-program, cooperation-management).
  • Student-Support / Help Desk: help our students with their daily questions regarding their studies and our platform.
  • Study Hubs: develop strategic partnerships all over Germany to partner with us to provide rooms locally for our students to study.
  • Regional Community: p lan student events, bring people together and help students in the region of Munich feel welcome and supported.
  • Mentoring-Programme: development of content with two of our partners, coordination and project management.

Your skillset:

  • You are a student and you share the vision of free education worldwide for refugees
  • You are fluent in English and German and are used to the MS Office Package
  • You are a self-starter and think like a social entrepreneur, you are an excellent communicator, and motivated to work hard

Our offer:

  • The opportunity to shape a social enterprise with your initiative and creative input
  • The insight in the development of a social start-up.
  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup and the chance to be part of a dynamic, international, and interdisciplinary team
  • Flexible working hours at one of our locations.

Ready to join our mission? Apply now!

We welcome your application including a short cover letter (max. 1 page), CV and availability in English or German to recruiting@kiron.ngo with the subject “StudentServices + your name”. For the final decision, we will also ask you to create a sample course matching. You will be supplied with all the relevant information as soon as you have turned in your application. 

Please keep in mind that we’re just allowed to hire interns who do a mandatory internship for their university (“Pflichtpraktikum”) or for their study orientation between bachelor and master (“Orientierungspraktikum”). With our limited budget we’re unfortunately unable to pay salaries.

If you don’t want to do a mandatory internship, Volunteers with a time investment of 10 hours per week are also welcome to apply via a typeform that you can find here: https://kiron.ngo/support/individuals.

The job does not fit your profile? Kiron is currently recruiting for 15 different paid positions, and many volunteering/ internship opportunities. More at www.kiron.ngo.
