The Kiron Buddy Program

The Kiron Buddy program brings together Kiron students with university students. The program aims to increase the study motivation and success of both the Kiron student and the university student. Buddies are expected to spend at least 20 minutes per week with their Kiron student.

  • Are you a university student and want to meet a Kiron student (offline or online)?

  • Want to support a Kiron student in their online studies?

  • Want to develop a buddyship of mutual learning and support?

  • Want to help a Kiron student learn the local language and let the Kiron student teach you her or his language?

  • Want to enjoy all kinds of activities with your buddy - from watching a soccer match to going to a concert?

  • Want to become part of the intercultural Kiron community?

Buddy Program at a glance:

  • Minimum time commitment: 20 minutes per week

  • Offline in France and Germany - Online anywhere in the world 

  • Core component of the program:

    • weekly check-in with your buddy - goal-setting and reflection (20 minutes)

  • Additional (optional) components of the program:

  • Language practice: teach each other your language!

  • Study sessions: study together!

  • Free-time and social activities: enjoy and have fun!

  • Supporting offers by Kiron

    • onboarding hangout: meet the Buddy Team and other buddies and Kiron students (online)

    • regular check-ins for buddies (online)

    • workshops to improve the program according to your feedback (offline)

    • community events (offline)
