Considering becoming a Kiron student? Great! Kiron wants to make it as easy as possible for you to get access to higher education and the entirety of our program is free of charge!

Note that we do not consider your grades or degrees from your previous studies . What matters most is that you are motivated and willing to seriously invest your time in your studies.

The next deadline to apply is 15th of October. 

In order to become a student at Kiron, we ask you to complete a four-part application process consisting of the following: personal information, motivation questions and an English language assessment to become a preliminary student. Once you are a preliminary student you need to complete two test MOOCs to become a full student at Kiron.

The first part of your application process will take about 30-50 minutes, plus the language assessment. 

Then to prove your status as a refugee, you will need to upload one of the following documents:

  1. residence permit (Aufenthaltserlaubnis)

  2. permission to reside (Aufenthaltsgestattung)

  3. a document that states the temporary suspension of deportation status (Duldung)

  4. the recognition of refugee status from any state

  5. the recognition of refugee status from UNHCR

  6. a document that states subsidiary protection status

  7. a document certifying that you have started your asylum application

  8. if you live in a refugee camp, a self-certification of your residence that is signed by the camp manager (please note that this document does NOT have any legal status and will only be used for our internal administrative processes)

  9. if you do not fit into any of the above categories, please fill in this certification and have it approved by a representative of a state organization/NGO that you are in contact with (for example, a social worker, counsellor, or legal consultant)

During the application process, we ask you to upload one of these documents as a scan or high-quality photo (sharp and high resolution). We accept documents in any European language, but English is preferred.

We will not share the document you upload with any external person or organization. It is required for our internal administrative processes only.

Once the document is uploaded, your personal information, motivation questions and English test are filled out, we will check your application. Once everything is in order you will be accepted as a preliminary student. Then you have one month to complete your Test MOOCs. Once you have completed the test MOOCs, you are a full student at Kiron.
