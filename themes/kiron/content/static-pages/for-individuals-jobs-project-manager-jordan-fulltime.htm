Project Manager

Start: November 2016 - Office: Amman/Berlin (fulltime)

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with a mission to remove the barriers refugees face in accessing higher education. Through an innovative model of blended learning, Kiron offers tailor-made curricula of MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) through renowned educational platforms like coursera and edx so that refugees can start studying, regardless of their asylum status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 60 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:.

  • Management of provided funds including monitoring and evaluation reporting and budgeting. 

  • Close coordination with Berlin and international teams. 

  • Manage the implementation of projects including a summer school and mentoring program.

  • Organisation and coordination of workshops and conferences for networking with project partners and presentation of interim results.

  • Communication with other NGOs, public sector organisations and higher education institutions. 

  • Consulting and supervising the project staff in the achievement of the project objectives and management related issues.

Your set of skills:

  • You hold a master’s degree in any relevant field of study. 

  • You have at least two years experience with third-party funded projects, including reporting and budgeting standards within educational or non governmental institutions.

  • You are experienced in the organisation and implementation of multiple projects.

  • You are experienced in managing and consulting (volunteer) teams.

  • You are a self-starter, adaptive & resilient and have an entrepreneurial mind-set.

  • You are fluent in English and Arabic or German and are proficient with the Microsoft Office Package and Google Apps.

  • You share the vision of free education worldwide for refugees.

Our offer:

  • The experience to lead a highly committed, international and interdisciplinary (volunteer) team.

  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup with several stakeholders (refugees, corporations, NGOs, higher education and public sector institutions).

  • Regular team building as well as representative events, mentoring, trainings and workshops.

  • A competitive salary within the social sector.

  • Flexible working hours (incl. Home Office) at our Amman location.
