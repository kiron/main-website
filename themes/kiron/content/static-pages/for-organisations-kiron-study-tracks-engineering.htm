Engineering

The Kiron Engineering Study Track lets our students acquire the knowledge of scientific and technical fundamentals in different fields of engineering and teaches an engineering approach to solving real-life problems. Upon completion of the core modules within the engineering field of their choice, they can choose to specialize in different subjects and target a specific Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Chemical or Environmental Engineering degree at a partner university.

These are followed by more technical foundations required for the specific engineering field of their choice. They cover subjects such as mechanics, materials science, thermodynamics, electronics, or geotechnology. Interdisciplinary skills in project management, quality management and law are also covered to different extents in the  engineering curricula.

After successfully completing one of the engineering tracks, our students will 

  • have gained the fundamental skills and mindset that any engineer must have in the first place,

  • be able to analyze real-world problems and approach them analytically,

  • be able to develop engineering models and apply them to solve actual engineering problems.
