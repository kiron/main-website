Option 3: Alternative paths to collaborate

Open a Study Hub to provide infrastructure
Kiron are always seeking more locations for Study Hubs to ensure our growing number of students have the resources to study online. 

Nominate staff for the Kiron Competence Pool
Through a competence pool of volunteers, Kiron aims to involve experts from the Partner Universities into the conceptual development and enhancement of Kironâ€™s activities as well as  assisting in critical evaluation.

Research about and with Kiron
The Kiron research department is dedicated to conducting scientific research within Kiron, with a focus on the topics of online education and forced migration. Our aim is to provide a way for individuals conducting research to further their own knowledge while simultaneously helping Kiron make scientifically informed decisions about its programs and methods.

We are building a network for scholars with an academic interest in Kiron and generating academic publications that can serve as valuable resources for those working on the topics of education and migration, whether in an academic or practical context.
