Donating to Kiron

Philanthropic support plays an integral role in the future development of Kiron. Even though we are seeking to establish sustainable revenue streams, the number of seats for Kiron students at our partner universities depends on the donations we receive during the first years of our project. Each place for a refugee to study a 3-year bachelor program for free is associated with a 3,000 Euro cost for Kiron.

In order to ensure the transparency of all donations coming in, we are collaborating with established Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) and refugee organisations to create external check-and-balances. In cooperation with our partners, Kiron publishes an annual evaluation, including the amount of donations received and the impact achieved.

Volunteering

Kiron is an international institution with students from more than 60 countries and an even more diverse range of cultural backgrounds. Our team reflects this international buzzing atmosphere. We work with people from all over the world, combining educational diversity and international experience with start-up expertise. Our team members and volunteers are one of our key strengths: everyone comes with diverse experiences, but is united by the same belief in the right to education. Kiron offers a range of volunteering and internship opportunities in our head office in Berlin, as well as in our teams in Boston, Istanbul and Munich. In many cases, it is also possible to work remotely from wherever you are located.

Kiron Student Services

The Kiron student service team helps students one-on-one with various difficulties they may encounter. If you would like to help a refugee in the process, please have a look at the ways you can help.

  • Kiron Buddies are the local contact persons for Kiron Students. Being a Buddy means introducing the Kiron student to the local student life and empowering them to overcome cultural and everyday challenges. Sign Up as a Buddy here.
  • Kiron Lecturers empower Kiron Students to learn the content of the subject that the lecturers are experts in. Being a lecturer means providing fixed lecture times each week that can be booked by up to five students for one topic. Sign up as a lecturer here.
  • Kiron Mentors support students either with a focus on their online studies or throughout vocational orientation with a focus on job market integration. Sign up to become a mentor here.
  • Kiron Psychosocial Counselors are supporting Kiron Students who are struggling to cope with hard experiences or situations. As specially trained experts with professional background they are the right contact people for students who need someone to talk to. Sign up as a psychosocial counselor here..
