Volunteering

Kiron offers a range of volunteering opportunities, e.g. helping our students with online tutoring or forum guidance, supporting the fundraising team in grant writing, designing communication materials, programming new platform features, organizing regional students events, matching curriculums and much more. Your support is needed at our German offices in Berlin and Munich, in our country teams in Istanbul, Paris and Amman as well as in our student regional groups in Aachen, Bielefeld, Cologne, Lüneburg and Passau. If you bring in special skills, e.g. programming or tutoring, it might also be possible to volunteer remotely from your location. 

Please help us find the right volunteer spot in our team by filling out this form and telling us more about your interests, skills and availability: Apply as a volunteer!

Internships

You are looking for an internship to improve your skills and to get insights in how a social start-up operates? You are creative, open-minded and a self-starter? We are always looking for talented interns to join our teams in the areas of Marketing/Communications, Human Resources, Business Development & Fundraising, Curriculum Development, Legal & Organisation, Event Management, Student Services in our Berlin and Munich offices.

Please check out our currently open internships or contact Alice with your initial application (CV+Motivation letter)!

(Please note that we can only offer internships if mandatory for your university studies -“Pflichtpraktikum”- or if meant as an orientation between studies -“Orientierungspraktikum”-)_With our limited budget we’re unfortunately unable to pay salaries.
