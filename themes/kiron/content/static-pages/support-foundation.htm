Support us as a foundation

As a non-profit charitable organisation, Kiron relies on the benevolence and generosity of third parties to sustain its mission and to make education accessible to refugees. As such, we are interested in foundations that would help fund Kiron in order to extend our reach and advance our quality.

Our Foundation donors receive recognition in our annual report and published donor listings, as well as regular updates through our newsletter, invitations to meetings with our executive director and staff, and invitations to Kiron events. We are also happy to send reports on specific areas of interest and the impact of your grant.


For more information, please contact:

Sophie Marquitan, Head of Business Development
