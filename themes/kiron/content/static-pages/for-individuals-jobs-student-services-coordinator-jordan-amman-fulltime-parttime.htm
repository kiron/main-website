Student Services Coordinator Jordan

Start: November 2016 - Office: Amman (full time/part time)

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with a mission to remove the barriers refugees face in accessing higher education. Through an innovative model of blended learning, Kiron offers tailor-made curricula of MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) through renowned educational platforms like coursera and edx so that refugees can start studying, regardless of their asylum status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 60 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:.

  • Coordination of a all activities and measures regarding Kiron’s student services in Jordan.

  • Quality assurance of the programme.

  • Optimisation of Kiron’s student services by developing and testing a student student needs and ensuring program is adapted to enhance student performance.

  • Monitoring and evaluation of programs.

  • Building a trusting relationship with the student body with an emphasis on clear and open lines of communication.

  • Ensuring students psychological needs are met.

  • Develop indicators for successful student services for Kiron students.

  • Liaise and report to staff in other countries, including the head office in Berlin.

Your set of skills:

  • You hold a bachelor's degree (master’s would be an asset) in the  field of psychology/ educational sciences or other relevant studies.

  • You possess profound knowledge of student support in Higher Education or in non-academic, pedagogic fields of action with at least one year of work experience in the Middle East.

  • You are experienced in managing (volunteer) teams. 

  • You have experience working one on one with refugees or disadvantage communities. 

  • You are a self-starter, adaptive & resilient and have an entrepreneurial mind-set.

  • You are fluent in academic English and Arabic and are proficient with the Microsoft Office Package and Google Apps.

  • You share the vision of free education worldwide for refugees.

Our offer:

  • The experience to lead a highly committed, international & interdisciplinary (volunteer) team.

  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup with several stakeholders (refugees, corporations, NGOs, higher education and public sector institutions).

  • Regular team building as well as representative events, mentoring, trainings and workshops.

  • A competitive salary within the social sector.

  • Flexible working hours (incl. Home Office)
