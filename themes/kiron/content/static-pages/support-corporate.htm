Kiron sustains strong relationships with the corporate sector - not in order to only to generate funding and non-financial support, but also to mutually examine the best ways to integrate skilled students and graduates into the international labour market. We are always looking to establish official partnerships and gain support from corporate organisations who contribute to Kiron’s mission in various ways: for example, granting scholarships or internships to our students, participating in corporate mentoring programs, establishing study hubs, and offering pro bono services.
