Internship Corporate Relations

Start: as soon as possible - Offices: Berlin - fulltime


Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with the mission to enable access to higher education and successful learning for refugees through digital solutions. As part of an innovative educational model Kiron offers coherent curricula using MOOCs (Massive Open OnlineCourses) from partner platforms like Coursera and edX so that refugees can start studying regardless of their status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 70 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:.

  • You shape Kiron’s future from a startup to a social enterprise by helping develop a sustainable business model also with the help of corporate partners.

  • You support the Corporate Relations Team with the development of their corporate contact base. This may include tasks and projects in corporate communications, the attendance of networking events, support with the acquisition of new partners, the searching and updating of new tools etc.

  • You work in a small team in a thriving social start up supported by a few motivated volunteers.

  • You take responsibility for projects depending on your preferences and skills and also have the possibility to take ownership of your own projects 

Your set of skills:

  • You share the vision of free education worldwide for refugees.

  • You are fluent in English and German and are used to the MS Office Package.

  • You already have first practical experiences in Business Development/Sales and/or have worked with NGOs, associations before. Ideally you have already developed your own network in the corporate world.

  • You are a self-starter and team player who is used to working independently and in small teams. 

  • You are business savvy and know how to professionally deal with business partners and how to establish new contacts.

Our offer:

  • The atmosphere and insights of an innovative and fast-growing social startup and the chance to be part of a dynamic, international, and interdisciplinary team where you can create real impact.

  • The opportunity to shape a social enterprise with your creative input and ideas, to develop your own professional network with interesting contacts and to get hands on experience within the NGO sector.

  • High level of autonomous working and a great diversity of projects.

Ready to join our mission? Apply now!

We are looking forward to your application via e-mail with the subject “Internship CR  + your name”. Include your cover letter, CV and availability in English or German in a single PDF file.
