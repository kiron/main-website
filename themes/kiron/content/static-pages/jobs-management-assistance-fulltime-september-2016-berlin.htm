Management Assistance

Start: September 2016 - Office: Berlin (full-time)

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with a mission to remove the barriers refugees face in accessing higher education. Through an innovative model of blended learning, Kiron offers tailor-made curricula of MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) through renowned educational platforms like coursera and edx so that refugees can start studying, regardless of their asylum status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 40 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:

  • Be the Assistant to our two Managing Directors in all matters of day-to-day operations.

  • Support the management and team in the coordination, communication and implementation of various aspects of the business routines.

  • Autonomously coordinate and execute organizational tasks such as scheduling meetings, booking travel arrangements and managing the contact database.

  • Prepare presentations and protocols for meetings and therefore facilitate workflows. 

  • Support in distributing targeted internal information and further optimizing the internal information flows.

  • Support in organizing conferences and internal events.

Your set of skills:

  • You are a self-motivated, pragmatic and solution-orientated personality with extraordinary organisational skills - and love to use those capabilities to ease the work of our top management.  

  • You have experience in the role of an assistant, ideally in a Start-up or other similar dynamic environment. 

  • Your are a team player and enjoy working in an international, young and dynamic team. 

  • You have a good understanding of the requirements of effective, efficient andsensitive business communication

  • You are an open, friendly and honest person who stands by his/her word. 

  • You bring in the flexibility for dealing with ad-hoc requests and for multi-tasking - as well as the ability to deliver results on short notice.

  • You are confident in using PowerPoint, Word and Excel and a quick-learner for google applications and all other organisational programs, such as Podio.

  • You have excellent German and English writing and skills.

Our offer:

  • The experience to work with a highly committed, international and interdisciplinary team.

  • The fun and inspiring atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup with several stakeholders (refugees, corporations, NGOs, higher education and public sector institutions).

  • Regular team building and representative events, mentoring, trainings and workshops.

  • A competitive salary within the social sector.
