Business Incubator Community Manager Brussels

Start: May 2017  - Offices: Brussels - Part-time (75%)

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with the mission to enable access to higher education and successful learning for refugees through digital solutions. As part of an innovative educational model Kiron offers coherent curricula using MOOCs (Massive Open OnlineCourses) from partner platforms like Coursera and edX so that refugees can start studying regardless of their status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 70 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Kiron is currently working on a launch of an EU-funded project that aims at facilitating, fostering and strengthening migrant entrepreneurs through light incubation cycles supported by mentors.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:

  • Launching a call for ideas and managing all PR-related activities 

  • Outreach to and recruitment of participants and mentors

  • Building a strong community to ensure mutual learning and support among participants

  • Event organisation and coordination (e.g. training sessions, workshops and mentor matching events)

  • Providing support to participants (e.g. liaison with mentors and providing  access to relevant networks)

Your set of skills:

  • You are an entrepreneur and you share the vision of free education worldwide for refugees

  • You have completed a bachelor’s or master’s degree in a relevant field

  • Knowledge of business incubation programs; marketing/salesmanship principles

  • Applicable knowledge of legal  and financial aspects for SMEs and startups; experience desired in any area of small businesses

  • Excellent demand of French and English required, Dutch is a plus

Our offer:

  • The opportunity to shape a project focusing on entrepreneurship for refugees and migrants with your initiative and creative input

  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup and the chance to be part of a dynamic, international, and interdisciplinary team

  • Flexible working hours.
