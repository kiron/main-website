Work with us

Kiron is an international institution with students from more than 60 countries and an even more diverse range of cultural backgrounds. Our team reflects this international buzzing atmosphere. We work with people from all over the world, combining educational diversity and international experience with start-up expertise. Our team members and volunteers are one of our key strengths: everyone comes with diverse experiences, but is united by the same belief in the right to education.
