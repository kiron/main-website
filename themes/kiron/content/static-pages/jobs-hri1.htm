Internship Human Resources

Start: January 2017 or later - Duration: min. 3 months - Office: Berlin

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization with a mission to remove barriers to access higher education for displaced people, asylum seekers, and refugees. Our goal is to empower young people through higher education degrees. After studying online for two years, Kiron students spend their third year of study at one of our partner universities and, through the cooperation with our partner schools, are able to earn an accredited university degree. As a growing social startup based in Berlin with regional chapters in Munich, Aachen, London, Paris, Brussels, Istanbul, and Boston, we are working hard on the realization of this concept. In October 2015, the first 1250 students began their online courses. With our core team of over 70 people as well as over 400 volunteers, and the cooperation of about 50 professors worldwide, we are driving the mission to give refugees around the world an opportunity to pursue their higher education careers.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:

  • Supporting our Human Resource Team in various upcoming projects for 2017
  • Assisting the management of our employee’s life cycle e.g. onboarding process, career development
  • Helping with preparation and conduct of team trainings and events
  • Implementing together with our HR Team a new organisational Model (“Holacracy”)
  • Developing strategic target & performance management tools and processes for human resources and our organizational culture

Your skillset:

  • You share the vision of free education worldwide for refugees

  • You are either enrolled as a student, ideally pursuing a degree with a focus in HR, or you are in your study orientation between bachelor and master

  • You are a self-starter, hard-working, adaptive and have an entrepreneurial mind-set 

  • You are fluent in English and German and proficient with the MS Office Package

  • You are highly pro-active and with the ability to prioritize and multitask

  • High interest in interacting with people and getting to know organisational processes

Our offer:

  • The opportunity to shape a social enterprise with your initiative and creative input

  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup and the chance to be part of a dynamic, international, and interdisciplinary team

  • Flexible working hours at our office in Berlin 

  • Investment in you: Regular team building and representative events, mentoring, trainings and workshops

We are looking forward to your application via e-mail with the subject “Intern_HR + your name” to Georgia Gkoumasi at internship@kiron.ngo. Include your cover letter, CV and availability in English or German in a single PDF file.

Please note that due to our limited budget as an nonprofit organization we are unfortunately unable to pay salaries. Therefore we are only offering mandatory internships/ educational program (“Pflichtpraktikum”) or study orientation internships between bachelor and master (“Orientierungspraktikum”).

If you want to support Kiron but none of the above mentioned internship types fit your current status, please get in touch with us and we can tell you more about volunteering options.
