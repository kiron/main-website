Kiron is currently looking for an Intern in Human Resource Management to support our operations in Berlin or Munich - Start: 15th of july or later.

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization with a mission to remove barriers to access higher education for displaced people, asylum seekers, and refugees. Our goal is to empower young people through higher education degrees. After studying online for two years, Kiron students spend their third year of study at one of our partner universities and, through the cooperation with our partner schools, are able to earn an accredited university degree. As a growing social startup based in Berlin with regional chapters in Munich, Aachen, London, Paris, Brussels, Istanbul, and Boston, we are working hard on the realization of this concept. In October 2015, the first 1000 students began their online courses. With our core team of over 60 people as well as over 200 volunteers, and the cooperation of about 50 professors worldwide, we are driving the mission to give refugees around the world an opportunity to pursue their higher education careers.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:

  • Recruiting: developing job descriptions and person specifications, preparing and publishing job adverts and selecting candidates
  • Volunteer and Intern management: recruiting and onboarding of volunteers, developing fringe benefit models, establishing a Kiron team culture 
  • Assisting the HR Manager in the implementation of a new organisational model (“Holacracy”)
  • Developing strategic Target & Performance Management tools and processes for Human Resource Development

Your skillset:

  • You are a student and you share the vision of free education worldwide for refugees
  • You are fluent in English and German and are used to the MS Office Package
  • You already have first practical or theoretical experiences in Human Resource Management
  • You are a self-starter and think like a social entrepreneur, you are an excellent communicator, and motivated to work hard 

Our offer:

  • The opportunity to shape a social enterprise with your initiative and creative input
  • The insight in the development of a Human Resource department in a social start-up.
  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup and the chance to be part of a dynamic, international, and interdisciplinary team
  • Flexible working hours at one of our locations.

We welcome your application including a short cover letter (max. 1 page), CV and availability in English or German to recruiting@kiron.ngo with the reference word “HRintern” and your name. For more information on Kiron have a look at our website (https://kiron.ngo/) or contact recruiting@kiron.ngo for questions concerning this advertisement.

Please keep in mind that we’re just allowed to hire interns who do a mandatory internship for their university (“Pflichtpraktikum”) or for their study orientation between bachelor and master (“Orientierungspraktikum”). With our limited budget we’re unfortunately unable to pay salaries. If you don’t want to do a mandatory internship, please get in touch with us and maybe we can find a volunteering solution in our HR department.
