At Kiron Business School students can graduate in Business Administration taking courses from the most renowned business schools in the world. School of Engineering

Kiron School of Engineering will allow students to combine theoretical fields such as mathematics and science and implement them into physical applications, to solve real life problems and bring ideas into reality. Studying engineering at Kiron will be the first step into an enriching field full of possibilities.

School of Architecture

Kiron School of Architecture is designed to foster the capability to substantially improve the built environment. Students will be concerned with the conceptualisation and design of individual buildings as well as urban configurations. Emphasis will be placed on learning how to include existing and emerging social, economic and technical demands into all stages of work.
