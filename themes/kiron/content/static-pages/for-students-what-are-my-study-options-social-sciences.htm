Social Sciences

Do you find yourself talking about politics with your friends? Are you passionate about giving a helping hand to others? Has politics or social work always interested you? We would recommend you to take one of our Social Sciences study tracks then.

Political Science

Political Science deals with the process of political decision-making in modern societies. In this study track, you will learn how political processes work in states, private companies and civil societies. Political Scientists competently use scientific theories and methods to identify and analyze political scenarios and challenges. 

As part of the Kiron core curriculum in Political Science you will gain foundational competencies in fields such as sociology, globalization, international development, and policy analysis. 

Key Learning Outcomes

After successfully completing the Political Science core curriculum, you

  • Understand what Political Science is about and what it can be used for

  • Have foundational knowledge in both political theory and research practice in Political Science

  • Have a critical, theoretically profound and scientifically driven perspective on global political challenges 

Where will the degree take you?

Once you’ll complete your studies at one of our partner universities you can pursue a career in various fields, such as governments and ministries, international organisations, public institutions, media and PR, consulting and many more. 

Social Work

There is an increasing interest and understanding of the question, “how social sciences can be used to solve practical problems?”. Our study track “Social Work” will make you an academically trained, hands-on expert in solving social problems. With a degree in Social Work, you will be empowered to act in ways that are scientifically profound, responsible and professional.

Social Work programs usually have different focuses. The Kiron core curriculum provides students with foundational competencies in the fields of human sciences, social justice, interculturality and diversity. 

Key Learning Outcomes

After successfully completing the Social Work core curriculum, you

  • Understand what Social Work is about and what it is good for

  • Have foundational knowledge of social theories related to Social Work as an academic discipline

  • Have a basic understanding of Social Work methods

  • Particularly have a foundational understanding of Health as a Social Work field

Where will the degree take you?

Once you have completed your studies at one of our partner universities you can pursue a career in various fields, such as health care, drug prevention, information centres, youth welfare, social welfare, HR, family centres and nursery schools, work with the disabled and many more.
