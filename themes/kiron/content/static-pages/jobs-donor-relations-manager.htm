Donor Relations Manager

Start: October 2016 - Office: Berlin - part-time

Kiron Open Higher Education (gGmbH) is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 with a mission to remove the barriers refugees face in accessing higher education. Through an innovative model of blended learning, Kiron offers tailor-made curricula of MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) through renowned educational platforms like coursera and edx so that refugees can start studying, regardless of their asylum status. Courses are mostly provided in English, completely free of charge for the students and all accessible via our learning platform Kiron Campus.

Through strong partnerships with accredited universities worldwide, students have the opportunity to finish their program with a regular bachelor’s degree. Today, a team of 40 employees receives further support from about 400 volunteers worldwide and a competence pool consisting of experts from relevant fields, such as professors, psychologists and business specialists.

Your opportunities and responsibilities at Kiron:.

  • Representing Kiron towards our supporters and donors

  • Creating and conducting donation campaigns

  • Responding to donor inquiries

  • Opportunity to work creatively and with teams in other countries

Your set of skills:

  • Required experience in: Communication, knowledge in (online) marketing is a plus

  • You are interested in: Project management, communication, story telling, working in an international team

  • Ability to work independently and conscientiously

  • Personal characteristics: Responsible, self-reliant, open minded

  • You’re fluent in German and English

Our offer:

  • 450€ monthly salary, 45 hrs/month, flexible working hours

  • Opportunity to shape a social enterprise with your initiative and creative input

  • Insight in the development of a Business Development department in a social start-up.

  • The atmosphere of an innovative and fast-growing social startup and the chance to be part of a dynamic, international, and interdisciplinary team

  • Attractive office location in Berlin-Mitte
