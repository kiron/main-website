Contact Kiron

Questions, comments, offers, requests, donations, press inquiries, anything else? Please contact us! Here are some of the people you may need to contact:

  • If you have questions about becoming a student, please have a look at our information for prospective students, or contact our student services team by email.
  • To sign up as a Kiron volunteer, please fill in this short application. If you have questions about volunteering, please contact Luise Müller.
  • For press inquiries or interview requests, please contact our press team.
  • For corporate inquiries please contact Sophie Marquitan. You can also find more information here.
  • For academic inquiries, please contact Hila Azadzoy. You can also find more information here.
  • For non-profit and NGO related questions, please contact Nora Hauptmann. You can also find more information here.
  • For technological questions, please contact Adam Roe.
  • For questions about donations, please contact Sophie Marquitan. You can also donate directly to us.
  • If you want to do research with our team or write a thesis with Kiron, please contact Lisa Hillers.

For anything else or general inquiries, please contact us here.
