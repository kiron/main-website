Business & Economics

The Business & Economics core curriculum offers the best of both worlds: In our Economics modules, students will understand on a theoretical level how modern economies function and integrate into a larger social framework, while in our Business modules they will learn on a practical level how to lead a business organisation. Going through our Business & Economics track and completing a degree at a partner university equips our students with a set of both intellectual and hands-on skills that make them particularly valuable job market candidates.

Key Learning Outcomes

In this study track, our students begin by learning the basics of both Business Administration and Economics, including for example foundations in maths & statistics, marketing, accounting, human resources, and the forces that drive our economy on a macro and micro level.

After successfully completing the Business & Economics curriculum our students will be able to:

  • make informed decisions that secure sustainable business success in a complex and fast-paced environment,

  • apply economic theory to the solution of real-world economic problems, and

  • gain core transferable skills (e.g. using quantitative methods and model thinking) that help you succeed in any future career.
